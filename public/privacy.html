Privacy Policy

At .Gap Mail, we value your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle information on our platform.

1. Temporary Email Usage

.Gap Mail provides temporary, disposable email addresses that are automatically generated and expire after a short period. These emails are not meant to be private or secure for long-term use.

2. Data Collection

  • We do not ask for or store any personal information from our users.
  • We may use basic analytics to understand how the site is used (e.g., page visits, session durations), but no identifying data is collected.

3. Email Message Handling

Email messages are visible in the browser only and are not stored or logged by us. They are provided temporarily and may be deleted after a short time automatically by the API service.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser’s local storage to save your generated email address and messages for convenience. This data never leaves your device.

5. Updates

This policy may be updated in the future. We encourage users to review this page occasionally for any changes.

6. Contact
