About .Gap Mail

.Gap (StopGap) Mail is a lightweight web-based tool that allows users to instantly generate a disposable, temporary email address. It can be used to receive verification emails, confirmation codes, or other one-time messages without revealing your real email address.

This tool is especially useful for:

  • Signing up for websites without using your personal email
  • Reducing spam in your main inbox
  • Testing email-based functionality as a developer

Features

  • Instantly generate a temp email address
  • Live polling for incoming messages
  • Browser notifications for new mail
  • Copy email address to clipboard
  • Simple, fast, and mobile-friendly UI
